    ABOUT US

    Established in the year 2063 BS, Balodaya Secondary School stands as a beacon of educational excellence in the heart of Sundar Bazar, Bheerkot-06, Syangja. Nestled in the scenic beauty of the region, our school is proud to be an integral part of the vibrant Bheerkot Municipality.
    At Balodaya, we believe in nurturing young minds and empowering them to reach their full potential. Our commitment to academic excellence, combined with a holistic approach to education, sets us apart as one of the best schools in Bheerkot Municipality. From Kindergarten to Grade 10, we provide a comprehensive educational journey that focuses on intellectual, social, and emotional development.
    Our dedicated team of experienced educators is passionate about creating a positive and inspiring learning environment. We strive to instill a love for learning in our students, encouraging them to explore their interests and cultivate critical thinking skills. Through a blend of traditional values and modern teaching methodologies, we aim to prepare our students for the challenges of the ever-evolving global landscape.
    As we continue to evolve and adapt to the changing educational landscape, Balodaya Secondary School remains steadfast in its mission to provide quality education and shape the future leaders of tomorrow. Join us on this exciting journey of learning and discovery at Balodaya Secondary School, where every day is an opportunity to grow, learn, and succeed.
